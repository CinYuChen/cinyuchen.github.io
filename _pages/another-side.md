While mathematics helps me explore structure and logic, art allows me to reflect on emotions and personal experiences. For me, art is a way of understanding both myself and the world.

Although these disciplines may seem different, both have taught me to appreciate abstraction, structure, creativity, and new ways of seeing the world. Some of my works are inspired by mathematical ideas and abstract structures, while others originate from thoughts, emotions, and questions that I have struggled with at different moments in life.

Rather than creating from the pain itself, I create from the understanding that emerges through reflection. Each artwork becomes both a record of growth and a reminder of the answers I once found for myself.

This page contains selected works from my artistic journey.
